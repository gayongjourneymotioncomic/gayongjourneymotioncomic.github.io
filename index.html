About Gayong Journey

"Gayong Journey" is a captivating story that centers around the transformative experiences of Rahmat and Naim. Rahmat, a timid teenager, finds himself being bullied until he encounters Naim, a skilled practitioner of Silat Gayong, a traditional martial art. Naim steps in to protect Rahmat and offers to teach him the ways of Silat Gayong. As they embark on their journey, Rahmat discovers newfound strength, confidence, and resilience through the art of Silat Gayong. Together, they navigate challenges, learn valuable life lessons, and form an unbreakable bond. "Gayong Journey" is a tale of empowerment, personal growth, and the power of mentorship, showcasing the transformative impact of Silat Gayong on the lives of its practitioners.
